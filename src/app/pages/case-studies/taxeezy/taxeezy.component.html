01

The Problem

UK tax compliance is notoriously fragmented. While the official HMRC portal provides the function, it lacks the supportive logic needed for complex cases like expatriate residency or landlord taxes. Users were faced with high cognitive load and a lack of real-time expert guidance.

Information Overload

Dozens of different tax "tramites" required a unified interface to prevent user fatigue.

Jargon Barrier

Legal and financial terminology needed a visual system that translates complexity into clarity.

Trust Gap

Managing personal finances requires a UI that communicates security and professionality

02

The Strategy

The core strategy was to build a One Digital Tax Hub. Instead of designing isolated screens, I architected a modular framework where every tax procedure —from self-employment to payroll— follows a consistent logical flow, powered by a custom Design System.

Systemic Standardization

Developed a common logic for data gathering across 10+ different tax categories.

Hybrid Support Model

Designed the seamless integration between automated tech flows and real-time expert chat.

Input Optimization

Reduced friction in complex forms through progressive disclosure and smart validation.

HMRC Alignment

Ensured the digital journey mirrored official requirements while drastically improving usability.
