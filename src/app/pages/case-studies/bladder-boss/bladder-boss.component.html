BladderBoss

Mobile App 路 Health 路 UX/UI

Designing a mobile experience to help users manage bladder health.

Context

BladderBoss is a health-focused mobile application designed to help individuals manage their bladder health more effectively. The app provides tools to track symptoms, hydration levels and daily habits, enabling a proactive approach to personal wellness.

The Challenge

  • Complex medical information for non-expert users
  • Need for simple and reassuring UX
  • Multiple data inputs and daily tracking
  • Balancing health accuracy with usability

My Role

I worked as UX/UI Designer, focusing on designing intuitive user flows and clear interfaces for a sensitive health-related product.

  • User flows and feature definition
  • Low and mid-fidelity wireframes
  • UX patterns for mobile health apps
  • Collaboration with product and development teams

The solution

The solution focused on creating a calm, supportive and easy-to-use experience, helping users track their health data without feeling overwhelmed.

Onboarding flow to introduce core features and set expectations.

Module screen with training and assets.

Profile screen easy to navigate.

Daily symptom tracking with simplified inputs.

Design decisions

  • Used simple language and visual hierarchy to reduce cognitive load.
  • Prioritised mobile-first UX patterns.
  • Designed flows to encourage daily engagement without pressure.
  • Focused on accessibility and emotional comfort.

Outcome

The project resulted in a clear and user-friendly wireframe system that defined the core experience and served as a solid foundation for further product development.
