BladderBoss

Mobile App 路 Healthcare 路 Product design

Designing a mobile product to support users in managing bladder health through daily tracking and behavioral insights.

Context

BladderBoss is a health-focused mobile product aimed at helping individuals manage bladder health more effectively. The app enables users to track symptoms, hydration levels and daily habits, supporting a more conscious and proactive approach to personal wellbeing.

The problem

  • Medical information was complex for non-expert users
  • Users needed a reassuring and non-judgmental experience
  • Daily tracking required multiple data inputs
  • Health accuracy had to be balanced with usability

My role

I worked as Product Designer, contributing to product discovery and defining the user experience for a sensitive healthcare domain.

  • User journeys and feature definition
  • Low and mid-fidelity wireframes
  • UX patterns for mobile health products
  • Collaboration with product and development teams

The solution

The solution focused on designing a calm, supportive and low-friction experience, helping users track their health data without feeling overwhelmed or stressed.

Onboarding flow to introduce core features and set user expectations.

Module screen with training content and educational assets.

Profile area designed for easy access and clarity.

Daily symptom tracking with simplified and accessible inputs.

Key product decisions

  • Used simple language and clear visual hierarchy to reduce cognitive load
  • Prioritised mobile-first patterns for daily usage
  • Designed flows to encourage engagement without creating pressure
  • Focused on accessibility and emotional comfort

Outcome

The project resulted in a validated product concept and a complete wireframe system defining the core user experience.

The design provided a solid foundation for further development and stakeholder alignment around future features.
