MOBILE HEALTHCARE SOLUTION

BladderBoss

Designing a supportive digital companion to manage bladder health through behavioral insights and low-friction tracking.

Product Design · Mobile Health (mHealth) · Behavioral UX

This case study explores the transition from clinical complexity to user empathy, focusing on the design system and wireframing process.

Role

Senior Product Designer

Goal

Behavioral Insights & Accessibility

Platform

iOS & Android Ecosystem

01

The Problem

Bladder health is a sensitive topic often wrapped in medical jargon. The challenge was to design a tool that felt supportive rather than clinical, encouraging long-term engagement in a high-friction tracking environment.

Medical Complexity

Complex healthcare data needed to be translated for non-expert users without losing accuracy.

Data Friction

Daily symptom tracking required multiple inputs that could easily lead to user burnout.

Emotional Stigma

Users needed a non-judgmental experience to manage a sensitive health condition comfortably.

02

The Strategy

The strategy focused on a Calm UI approach: reducing cognitive load through visual hierarchy and simplified language. I defined the core experience through a modular wireframe system, ensuring the product could scale from tracking to educational modules.

Discovery & Mapping

Defined user journeys focused on emotional safety and accessibility.

Wireframe System

Created low to mid-fidelity prototypes to validate medical data flows.

Calm UI Patterns

Developed interaction patterns that encourage engagement without creating pressure.

Technical Handoff

Collaborated with developers to ensure the behavioral triggers were programmatically feasible.

03

Design Foundations

I built a comprehensive wireframe library and a foundational design system to ensure consistency across the onboarding, tracking, and educational modules.

04

The Impact

The project established a validated user experience foundation, ready for global launch and medical certification.

Validation

Product concept and wireframe system fully validated with healthcare stakeholders.

Accessibility

Established a high-contrast, easy-to-read UI optimized for diverse user demographics.

Clarity

Simplified clinical data entry, significantly reducing user drop-off during daily logs.
